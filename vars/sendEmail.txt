<strong>sendEmail(String state, String emailaccount)</strong>
<p>
    Sends an email to with the state header and to the selected email account.
</p>
<p>
    <strong>Defaults:</strong>
    <ul>
        <li>state: 'failed'</li>
        <li>emailaccount: 'myemail@myemail.com'</li>
    </ul>
</p>
